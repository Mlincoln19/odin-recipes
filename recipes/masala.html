Tikka Masala

Tikka Masala is an incredible dish and everyone, in my opinon should try it at least once. As such here is an incredibly simple recipe that relies on preprepared frozen Masala you can get at the store.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Tikka Masala
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Frozen Naan
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Put Frozen Tikka Masala in pot/pan on medium heat to let thaw and warm.
  2. Preheat oven to package instructions for the frozen naan
  3. Melt butter with garlic on medium-high heat until garlic is golden brown
  4. Once Tikka Masala is fully thawed and lightly boiling add heavy cream
  5. Brush Naan with garlic butter and cook to package instructions
  6. Take the Tikka Masala off heat once it has started to boil and cover until serving
  7. Put Naan on a plate for easy access once finished cooking and serve
